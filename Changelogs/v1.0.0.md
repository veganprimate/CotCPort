# CotCPort hard mode `v1.0.0`

## Developer changelog
This is the first build that I consider a full release rather than a pre-release snapshot.

Compared to the pre-release export, `v1.0.0` grows from **157** files to **7820** files. In concrete terms, that means:

- **7665** added assets
- **82** shared assets revised
- **2** obsolete assets removed

The release is defined by four large shifts:

- I expanded the port from a mostly database-focused package into a broad **quest / event / NPC / world-state** integration.
- I added **several custom units** with complete skillboards.
- I pushed the hard mode side much further with a near-global **enemy, break, reward, and encounter rebalance**.
- I did a broad **multilingual text pass** so the imported content actually surfaces cleanly in Japanese, English, Korean, Simplified Chinese, and Traditional Chinese.

### Pierro mechanical identity

Pierro is designed around setup-sensitive payoffs rather than raw front-loaded damage:

- `Masquerade Edge` rewards attacking into `Blind` or `Accuracy Down`, applies `Enchant`, and gives Pierro a 1-turn evade-and-retaliate posture.
- `Intermission` is a strong self-stabilizer rather than a free reset: it gives large self-heal, high evasion, and one physical dodge, but still commits Pierro to a 2-turn sleep state.
- `Tri-Edge Encore` turns `Enchant` into a loop engine by restoring `1 BP` on use and hitting harder into enchanted targets.
- `Blinding Flurry` is his dedicated single-target pressure skill for extending blindness play.
- `Misdirect` is a clean accuracy-control tool and also feeds his evade retaliation posture.
- `Veiled Refrain` lowers `Accuracy` and `Evasion` while also forcing the target to the end of the turn order for 2 turns.
- `Blackout Ovation` gives him a real all-target blind pressure option. Great for encounters in the hard-mode version of the mod.
- `White Grape Wine` is not a generic heal. It is an ultimate-level single-target recovery tool that also extends allied positive timed effects and shortens negative timed effects based on Boost.

The passives are built to support that entire loop:

- `Backstage Sommelier` extends augmenting effects on heal targets and on Pierro himself.
- `Induction Step` extends attribute-down effects when Pierro applies them and again when he returns from the back row.
- `Choreographic Proof` extends ailment duration and refunds SP when Pierro acts into break windows.
- `Defiant Ad-lib` improves ailment resistance and turns existing debuffs on Pierro into extra BP recovery.

### Kit mechanical identity

The board is deliberately built on self-sabotage:

- `Panicked Spread Rush` lowers Kit’s own `Accuracy` before the multi-hit all-target lance sequence starts.
- `Fearful Rapid Draw` lowers Kit’s own `Phys. Atk.` and `Phys. Def.` before firing its bow string.
- `Feigned Bravery` raises front-row `Phys. Atk.` while also dropping front-row `Phys. Def.` for longer.
- `Blinding Arrow Flash` is a real risk/reward screen-clearer because it can also blind Kit unless used at max Boost.
- `Death's Embrace` converts self-inflicted `Poison` and `Bleeding` into a full-party defensive window.
- `Accursed Bloodline` is a proper cursed ultimate: it gives the whole party a 50% omni-offense / omni-defense / speed / crit / evasion spike for 1 turn, but Kit receives `Death Sentence` based on ultimate level and the effect is deliberately marked as non-extendable.

The support skills reinforce the intended asymmetry:

- `Calming Presence` creates a very narrow but meaningful cleric-pair synergy.
- `Bloodline Awakening` gives Kit a hard light weakness and a strong dark mitigation hook.
- `Newbie Wanderer` intentionally pushes him away from being a clean stat stick.
- `Desperate Soul` gives him different damage incentives depending on row position.

## Enemy rebalance (hard mode specific)

### Global enemy pass

`/Content/Local/DataBase/Enemy/EnemyID` changes **all 8000 rows**.

The heaviest field concentrations are:

- `m_BreakRate`: **7993** rows (decreased the break damage bonus from 100% more damage to roughly 60% more damage)
